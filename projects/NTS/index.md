## Taking Notes
- After unlocking your profile you have three options below your note block.
  Save, Lock, and Clear.

-Save: Lets you save what you have typed in the note block.

-Lock: Returns you to the PIN screen.

-Clear: Removes all notes. 
> [!IMPORTANT]
> The "Clear" action is UNREVERSABLE. Only use if neccesary.

## Account Help

-To delete a user you must delete the localstorage of your web browser. This is not the same as cookie data.

-Recovering a users PIN is not possible.

-All data is stored locally on your device. No servers store your data and no accounts are needed. Remember that because of this clearing your local browser data deletes ALL notes.
